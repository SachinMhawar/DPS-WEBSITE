About Us

Located in the very heart of Gurugram on Dwarka Expressway, Delhi Public School, Sector 102A & 103 falls under the aegis of DPS Society, New Delhi. It is listed among the Best Schools in Gurugram. A trusted name in the field of quality education, it is widely recognised across the globe for its progressive approach, commitment to excellence and adopting the highest standards of safe, secure and stimulating environment. The school provides ample opportunities for the holistic development of the children, along with instilling a sense of critical thinking in the young learners.

A vibrant & tech-savvy institution with state-of-the-art campus, DPS Dwarka Expressway caters to every need of the new generation. Brilliantly designed modern classrooms, ultra-modern infrastructure and a wholesome atmosphere provide a motivating and exhilarating environment for the learners to build and grow. It is a place where innovative teaching methods meet cutting-edge technology, creating a dynamic environment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and a lifelong love for learning. The school's commitment to holistic education ensures that academic excellence is complemented by the development of emotional intelligence, social responsibility, and physical well-being. It is not just a place of education but a launching pad for future leaders, thinkers, and compassionate global citizens.

The school represents the transformation of education globally. Driven by latest research, the school uses innovative teaching methodology with special emphasis on extra-curricular and co-curricular activities and communication skills. Values form an intrinsic part of the curriculum. We strive towards building a positive attitude in our students with our rich and varied heritage.
