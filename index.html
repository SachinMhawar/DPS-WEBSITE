DPIS SOCIETY

The DPIS Society is a non-profit, non-proprietary private educational organization. This network of English medium, co-educational, secular schools provides education from Pre-Nursery/Nursery to Class XII. The DPIS Rewari Suncity school, as part of this family, is not merely an institution; it represents a network of values, systems, and relationships that shape holistic learning and growth.

www.dpsfamily.org

ABOUT US

The school represents the transformation of education at DPIS Rewari Suncity. Driven by the latest research, the school employs innovative teaching methodologies with special emphasis on extra-curricular and co-curricular activities, along with developing strong communication skills. Values form an intrinsic part of the curriculum. We strive to build a positive attitude in our students while nurturing them with our rich and varied heritage.
